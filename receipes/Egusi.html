Egusi

Description

Pounded Yam and egusi soup recipes are very popular Nigerian cuisine that originated in the Kano

Ingredients

  • Tuber yam
  • Yam flour
  • 1 Pound Boneless chicken ( Cut in bite-size pieces)
  • 1 Medium Sliced Onion
  • 2 pieces Maggi Cubes

    • Steps

    1. Boil water
    2. Add ground melon to a bowl, add about a cup of water and mix to form a paste then set aside. Wash and chop the spinach, drain in a colander and set aside.
    3. Place a big pan on medium heat, add palm oil and heat for about 3 minutes (Do not bleach oil) add the reserved chopped onions and Sautee till translucent
    4. Add pepper mix, locust beans and stir to combine. Bring to boil for 5 minutes.
    5. Remove the lid.
    6. Add smoked turkey, ground crayfish and crayfish.
    7. Add shredded smoked mackerel and gently stir into the soup.
